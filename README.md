# Innovation & Technology Alliance (ITA) – Interest Website

A modern, single-page landing website created for the **Innovation & Technology Alliance (ITA)** at the **University of Minnesota Crookston**.

This website serves as the official interest page for the proposed student organization and allows students to learn about the club and submit their interest through a Google Form.

---

## About ITA

The Innovation & Technology Alliance (ITA) is a proposed student organization focused on:

* Software Development
* Artificial Intelligence (AI)
* Cybersecurity
* Web & Mobile Development
* Data Science
* Entrepreneurship
* Hackathons & Competitions
* Professional Development
* Networking & Collaboration

Our goal is to create a welcoming community where students from all academic backgrounds can explore technology, build projects, develop leadership skills, and prepare for future careers.
