What is ITA?

The Innovation & Technology Alliance (ITA) is a proposed student organization at the University of Minnesota Crookston designed to bring together students who are passionate about technology, innovation, entrepreneurship, and emerging technologies. Whether you're interested in software development, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, web development, data science, startups, or simply learning new skills, ITA aims to create a collaborative environment where students can learn, build, and grow together.
